🎮 Who Is This For?

FFF Diamonds is designed for Free Fire enthusiasts who love the thrill of spinning, collecting, and competing — without spending real money. It's a fun, casual entertainment experience inspired by the Free Fire universe.

📱 Platform & Technology

Built natively with Flutter for Android, FFF Diamonds delivers a premium app experience with smooth animations, a beautiful dark UI, and fast performance — all packaged in a small, lightweight APK.

Your Data

Privacy by design.

We believe in minimal data collection and full transparency. Here's exactly what we do and don't do.

💾 Data Storage

All your progress — diamond count, daily bonus, spin history — is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We never transmit your data to any server.

🙅 No Account Required

Zero sign-up. No email, no password, no social login. You open the app and play. That's it. No personally identifiable information is ever collected.

🛡️ No Sensitive Permissions

FFF Diamonds requests no camera, microphone, contacts, or location permissions. The only permission needed is internet access for future ad support.

👶 Children's Safety

This app is suitable for users aged 13+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. There are no in-app purchases or real-money elements.

📢 Advertising

The app may display advertisements via third-party networks. These may collect anonymous usage data per their own policies. We never sell your personal data to advertisers.

✉️ Contact Us

Questions about your privacy? Reach us at support@fffdiamonds.app — we aim to respond within 48 hours.
